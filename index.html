About

I am a PhD student working on theoretical physics, focusing on quantum field theory, gravity, and related topics. My supervisor is Toshifumi Noumi at Komaba High Energy Theory Group.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Open Effective Field Theory
  • Black Hole Physics
  • Inflation
  • Unitary Representation theory of Lie algebra for cosmology and quantum field theory
